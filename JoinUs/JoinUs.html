Committees

1) Social Committee: plans events, activities, surprises and bonding opportunities. Day to day operations include event and logistical planning, emailing, and working with the SAC as well as other organizations to help coordinate and facilitate events. It is a super fun way to apply organization and event planning skills!

2) The Marketing and Recruitment Committee: assists in recruiting new members, publicizing events and creating promotional material. This includes tabling, flyering, designing graphics, taking photos, creating albums/photo slideshows and posting on social media outlets (Facebook and GroupMe. Members should be able to use or be willing to learn to use digital media, take photos, and record videos. Maintaining a professional, energetic, and friendly attitude is a must. Will be working closely with Marketing Chairs, Recruitment Chairs and Historian.
